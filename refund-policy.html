Refund Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

We want you to be completely satisfied with BullBill. If you are not happy with your subscription, contact us within 7 days of a charge and we will make it right.

1. Free Trial

All new BullBill accounts come with a 30-day free trial. You will not be charged during this period. You can cancel before the trial ends with no cost whatsoever.

2. Refund Eligibility

You may request a full refund for your most recent monthly subscription charge if:

  • You contact us within 7 days of the charge date.
  • You are requesting a refund for the first time on your account.
  • Your account has not been terminated for a violation of our Terms of Use.

3. How to Request a Refund

Email us at support@bullbill.in with the subject line "Refund Request" and include:

  • Your registered email address.
  • Your business name.
  • The reason for the refund request.

We will respond within 2 business days and process approved refunds within 5–7 business days to your original payment method.

4. Non-Refundable Situations

  • Refund requests made more than 7 days after the charge date.
  • Partial months — subscriptions are billed monthly and are not pro-rated.
  • Accounts terminated for Terms of Use violations.
  • Charges from previous billing cycles (only the most recent charge is eligible).

5. Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings or by emailing us. Cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing period — you will retain access until then. We do not charge cancellation fees.

6. Contact

For refund or cancellation queries, reach us at support@bullbill.in or through our Contact page.
