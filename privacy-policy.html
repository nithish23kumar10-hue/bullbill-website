Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

BullBill Technologies ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of restaurant owners and their customers. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our billing and restaurant management software available at bullbill.in.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, business name, email address, phone number, and GSTIN when you register.
  • Business Data: Menu items, pricing, table configurations, orders, and billing records that you create within the app.
  • Customer Data: Phone numbers and loyalty points of your restaurant's customers, collected at billing with their consent.
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, device type, and IP address for improving our service.
  • Payment Information: Subscription billing details processed securely through our payment partners. We do not store full card numbers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the BullBill billing and restaurant management service.
  • To generate GST-compliant invoices and reports.
  • To power the customer loyalty and rewards features.
  • To send you important service notifications and support messages.
  • To improve the app based on usage patterns.
  • To process your subscription payments.

3. Data Isolation & Security

Your restaurant's data — sales figures, customer list, menu, and billing history — is completely isolated from all other restaurants on the platform. No other business can access your data. We use encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS) and at rest to protect your information.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: Third-party vendors (e.g., payment processors, SMS providers) who assist us in operating the service, bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by Indian law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfer: In the event of a merger or acquisition, your data may be transferred with prior notice.

5. Data Retention

We retain your business data for the duration of your active subscription and for 12 months after cancellation, to allow reactivation. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Customer's Privacy

When you collect a customer's phone number for the loyalty programme, you are responsible for informing them that their number will be stored for rewards purposes. BullBill processes this data on your behalf as a data processor under Indian law.

7. Cookies

Our website uses only essential cookies required for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

BullBill is a business tool intended for adults (18+). We do not knowingly collect information from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or an in-app notice. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For any privacy-related questions or requests, please contact us at:

  • Email: support@bullbill.in
  • Website: bullbill.in
